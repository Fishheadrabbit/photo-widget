Version 1.6 is released with the following updates:

New: Folder based widgets — choose a folder to have all its photos automatically added to a widget
New: Shuffle — flip through the widget photos in a random order
New: Disable auto-flipping — you can now disable the automatic flipping of widgets

This update also includes a bunch of small adjustments to the interface to better accommodate all controls

Feedback and suggestions are always welcome, visit the project page to contribute!
