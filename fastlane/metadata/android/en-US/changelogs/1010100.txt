Version 1.1.1 is released with the following updates:

New: Pick multiple photos at once when configuring a widget
New: Share photos directly from your gallery to create a new widget
New: Editing tools when configuring a widget to move, remove and crop photos
New: Click a widget on the home screen to enlarge the current photo

Fix: Adding widgets via the app not working correctly on some Samsung devices
Fix: Handling crashes that would sometimes happen when trying to import photos

Feedback and suggestions are always welcome, visit the project page to contribute!
