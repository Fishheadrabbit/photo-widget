Version 1.14 is released with the following updates:

* Update widgets at a scheduled time
* Restore recently removed photos (photo widget only, for photos deleted after
  version 1.14)
* The View in full screen tap action now offers a way to view the original
  photo
* Improved pre-crop image quality when importing photos
* Reduced in-app motion animations

Feedback and suggestions are always welcome, visit the project page to
contribute!
